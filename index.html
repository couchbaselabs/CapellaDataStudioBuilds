Capella DataStudio

A community supported, free, cool data management tool with a single pane of glass UI for Capella Operational, Capella Columnar and Couchbase Server Clusters

Couchbase Server

Connect to your Couchbase Server Clusters. These can be On-prem, deployed on any Cloud provider or Docker.

Capella Operational

Connect to your Capella Operational Clusters. Use the cool AI assisted iQ to generate your SQL++ queries.

Capella Columnar

Connect to your Capella Columnar Clusters. Use the cool AI assisted iQ to generate your SQL++ queries.
